Best Times to Visit

Japan is beautiful year-round, but each season offers a unique experience. Plan your trip to match what you want to see.

Spring (March-May)

Experience the world-famous cherry blossoms and pleasant weather perfect for sightseeing.

star Highlight: Cherry Blossom Festivals

Summer (June-August)

Join vibrant festivals, enjoy outdoor activities, and climb Mount Fuji during climbing season.

star Highlight: Traditional Summer Festivals

Autumn (Sept-Nov)

Witness the spectacular autumn colors as leaves turn vibrant shades of red, orange, and gold.

star Highlight: Autumn Illuminations

Winter (Dec-Feb)

Enjoy winter sports in Hokkaido, relax in hot springs, and experience magical snow festivals.

star Highlight: Sapporo Snow Festival
